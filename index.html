What is Sentiment Analysis?

🕵️‍♀️
Detective Senti
"Hello there, future AI detective! I'm Detective Senti, and I solve mysteries by understanding emotions in text. Want to learn my secrets?"

Imagine you could read people's minds and know exactly how they feel just by looking at what they write. That's what sentiment analysis does!

Every day, people write millions of messages, reviews, and posts online. Some are happy 😊, some are sad 😢, and some are just sharing facts 📝. Our job as emotion detectives is to figure out which is which!

How Does AI Understand Emotions?

🤖
AI Assistant Ana
"Great question! Let me show you the three main ways AI detects emotions in text."
📚

Method 1: Word Lists (Lexicon)

AI has lists of happy words and sad words. It counts them up!

Happy words: love, amazing, wonderful
Sad words: hate, terrible, awful
🔍

Method 2: Pattern Detection

AI looks for clues like !!! (excitement) or 😢 (sadness)

"WOW!!!" = Very excited
"why me... 😭" = Very sad
🧠

Method 3: Machine Learning

AI learns from millions of examples to understand context

Learns that "sick" can mean "cool" or "ill"
based on the context!
